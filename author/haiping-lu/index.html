Haiping Lu

Senior Lecturer in Machine Learning

University of Sheffield

Job: Postdoctoral Research Associate in Machine Learning for Medical Image Analysis. Deadline: 12th Jan 2022. Fixed term till 31st March 2023, start as soon as possible.

Scholarships: Faculty scholarship, CSC for Chinese applicants, and EPSRC DTP. Deadline: 5pm 26th Jan 2022. Email ONE PDF including CV (w/t GPA/rank), statement on why apply (1 page), papers or equivalents (min 1, max 3), and transcripts before applying. Only highly qualified candidates will be considered. Non-native English speakers need IELTS.

I am a Senior Lecturer in Machine Learning and AI Strategy Lead at the Department of Computer Science, and Insigneo Research Director for Healthcare Data / AI, University of Sheffield. I am also the Machine Learning Theme Lead for Sheffield in the N8 Centre of Excellence in Computationally Intensive Research.

My research focuses on developing translational AI technologies for better analysing multimodal data, particularly multidimensional data and heterogeneous graphs, in healthcare (medical imaging and drug discovery) and beyond. I lead the development of the PyKale library to provide more accessible machine learning from multiple sources for interdisciplinary research, officially part of the PyTorch ecosystem.

I was a recipient of an IEEE CIS Outstanding PhD Dissertation Award, a Hong Kong Research Grants Council Early Career Award, an AAAI Outstanding Program Committee Member Award, and an Amazon Research Award, and a joint-recipient of a Wellcome Trust Innovator Award and an NIHR AI in Health and Care Award. I am a Senior Member of IEEE. I served as an area chair of IJCAI2021, and a senior program committee member of IJCAI and AAAI since 2017.

Two of my MSc dissertation students received the Fretwell-Downing Prize for the best MSc Dissertation in their respective years (P. Bai in 2017 and H. Xu in 2019).
